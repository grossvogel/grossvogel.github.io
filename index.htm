What does it do?

A big chunk of your inbound convos are probably questions from prospective customers. The same questions, over and over.

Each one deserves a kind and well thought-out reply, but the repetition can drain your enthusiasm. Convo Keeper lets you store your best answers to frequently asked questions so you can quickly and easily re-use them next time. (And the time after that, and ...)

  • You won't dread opening your inbox, because the tedious messages just got super easy. The rest are probably customer appreciation photos!
  • You won't forget important details the 11th time you answer a question about international shipping. It's been on auto-pilot since the second time.
  • You can still personalize the Convo before you send it. The rote details are taken care of, so you can spend your time making a personal connection with (and awesome products for) your customers.

How does it work?

Once you install the plugin, it's easy to store your convos in one of two ways:

  1. Write your Etsy convo response normally. Before you send the message, click Save Convo to store the text.
  2. If you've already sent the convo you want to store, highlight that text and click Save Convo to store it.

To use a stored convo, just activate the plugin from the convo screen and click the one you want. The text will appear in the reply box as if you'd typed it yourself.
